Woodcutting

Cutting down trees in New World will raise your skill in lumberjacking. The higher your level rises in lumberjacking the different kinds of trees you will be able to cut down. The different types of wood are as follows:

  • Aged Wood
  • Green Wood
  • Wyrdwood
  • Ironwood

Mining

Mining is a useful harvesting skill that can be used to gather different types of ores. The following ores are listed here:

  • Iron Ore
  • Silver Ore
  • Gold Ore
  • Starmetal Ore
  • Orichalcum Ore
  • Platinum Ore

Fishing

Fishing is for those looking for a calm and peaceful resource gathering expierence. The more you fish you will level your fishing skill and unlock hidden fishing spots along with new fish to be caught.

Harvesting

While exploring the world of New World you will run into many different plants you can pick. Using a syckle the following plants can be harvested to use in crafting:

  • Barley
  • Carrott
  • Corn
  • Cranberry
  • Herb
  • Squash
  • Strawberry
